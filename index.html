Board Members

Raghul Vijayan

Founder of CIRPI Foundation

Raghul Vijayan is a Testing Associate at Cognizant with a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Tamil Nadu College of Engineering, Coimbatore. He is known for his dedication, kind-hearted nature, and commitment to making a positive impact on society. Driven by a passion for empowering students, Raghul volunteers actively to support their growth and future prospects. This vision led him to establish the CIRPI Foundation, which focuses on creating opportunities for underserved communities. Through his leadership, the foundation continues to inspire change and foster educational development. Raghul’s efforts exemplify his belief in building a brighter and more equitable future for all.


Gokul Kittusamy

Co-Founder of CIRPI Foundation

Gokul Kittusamy is an Executive Officer at Elarchitek and a passionate advocate for practical education. He holds a degree in Production Engineering from the prestigious Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai. Gokul dedicates his time to visiting government schools, introducing students to hands-on learning and tinkerable education. His goal is to bridge the gap between traditional teaching methods and real-world skills, fostering creativity and curiosity among young minds. As a co-founder of the CIRPI Foundation, he plays a key role in driving initiatives that promote innovative and accessible education for underserved communities. Gokul’s vision and tireless efforts continue to empower students and inspire them to dream bigger.


Kaviya Senthil

Secretary of CIRPI Foundation

Kaviya holds a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science from Government Arts and Science College, Coimbatore, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Political Policy from Chanakya University. With a strong passion for political policy and consultancy, Kaviya has dedicated his career to shaping impactful strategies for governance and public service. He gained valuable experience during his internship with a Member of Parliament and is now working as a Political Consultant in Mumbai. As the Secretary of the CIRPI Foundation, Kaviya brings his expertise and vision to drive initiatives that focus on policy advocacy and community development. Kaviya’s commitment to fostering societal change and his analytical approach to problem-solving make him an integral part of the foundation’s mission to empower and uplift communities.

Mentors

Preethi Damodharan

Advisor of CIRPI Foundation

Preethi Damodharan is a Software Engineer at Dell Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore, with a Master’s degree in Computer Technology from Vellore Institute of Technology. She is a passionate educator and mentor, dedicated to empowering young minds through practical learning. As the founder of Iyal Foundation, Preethi focuses on reaching government schools to provide students with hands-on education, helping them build skills and confidence to achieve their dreams. She also conducts classes to enhance their abilities, inspiring them to strive for excellence. Preethi’s unwavering commitment to education and mentorship continues to create a lasting impact, making her a true driving force behind the foundation’s mission to transform young lives.


Arumugam

Advisor of CIRPI Foundation

Arumugam sir is a dedicated school teacher in a government school, deeply committed to the betterment of students’ lives. He actively collaborates with various organizations to create opportunities for students and enhance their educational experiences. With a focus on academic excellence, sir constantly motivates students to prepare for competitive exams like NEET and JEE. He also provides career guidance, empowering them with the knowledge to make informed decisions and achieve their goals. Arumugam sir's unwavering dedication to mentoring young minds makes him an inspiration and a driving force in shaping brighter futures.


Srinivasan

Advisor of CIRPI Foundation

Srinivasan is a dedicated professional with years of experience as a Bank Manager. Though retired, his passion for serving the community remains unwavering. He is an active volunteer with the Rotaract Club of Tambaram, where he manages the club’s accounts and provides guidance to ensure its smooth functioning. Beyond his work with the club, Srinivasan offers valuable support to various organizations, helping them enhance their efforts for societal betterment. His commitment to service and his expertise continue to make a meaningful difference in the lives of many.
